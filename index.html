About Me

I am a Research Trainee at INMAS, DRDO, focusing on nuclear physics applications in radiopharmaceutical metrology and internal dosimetry. My academic training includes an MSc in Physics (Nuclear specialization) from University of Delhi and hands-on experience at IUAC.

During my Master's dissertation, I studied collective excitations and triaxial deformation in the 127Cs nucleus using the INGA HPGe Clover array. I developed Python-based automation tools for gamma-ray spectral deconvolution, peak fitting, background subtraction, and efficiency correction.

Interests: Exotic nuclei, fission fragments, nuclear reactions, detector development, experimental spectroscopy, many-body dynamics, and nuclear structure.
